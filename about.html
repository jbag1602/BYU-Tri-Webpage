About


BYU Triathlon is a group of student athletes at Brigham Young University dedicated to interests concerning the sport of Triathlon. As the officially affiliated university triathlon organization, BYU Triathlon is currently organized as a BYUSA club. Being the official representative for the school, our athletes compete with other university triathlon teams in the USA Triathlon Collegiate Club division.


BYU Triathlon is dedicated to promoting, teaching, and competing in the sport of triathlon.
