Schedule

BYU Triathlon competes in two major races per season.

The major fall race that the team competes in is the Pumpkinman Triathlon in Boulder City, Nevada, occurring in late October. The major spring race that the team competes in is the Lake Havasu Triathlon, located in Lake Havasu City, Arizona, in late March. Havasu is also the regional MCTC conference championship for the USAT Collegiate Club division.


The team also intermitently competes in smaller race series such as the Kearns Indoor Triathlon Series, the Elevate indoor Triathlon, the Rex Lee Run and the Top of Zion's relay.
